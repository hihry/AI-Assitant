{jd_requirements}

SCORE 1 — EXACT SCORE (0-100)

- Weak verbs: assisted, helped, supported, contributed to, worked on, participated in
- Score = (strong verb bullets / total project bullets) × 100, rounded
- List every verb found and classify it
